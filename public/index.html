Get Notifications: On Chrome (Android) → tap ⋮ menu → Add to Home Screen → install → open → tap Allow when prompted. You'll be notified when someone replies to your confession! 🔔

Share as Screenshot: Tap Share on any confession → choose Instagram or WhatsApp. We generate a purple-red story card screenshot automatically so you can post it quickly.

🗑️

REMOVE! Button: If a confession is inappropriate or offensive, click REMOVE! to flag it. When 4 users remove the same confession, it gets hidden from the feed for admin review. Your privacy is protected — admins never see who clicked remove.
